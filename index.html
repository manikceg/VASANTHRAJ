About

I am Vasanthraju — a visual storyteller drawn to the sacred, the street, and the quietly extraordinary. My work wanders across festivals, temples, and forgotten lanes, seeking the moments where tradition breathes and light tells the truth.

Rooted in South India yet restless by nature, I document culture not as a spectator, but as a wanderer who believes every face holds a universe and every ritual deserves a witness.

Wonder and Wander.
